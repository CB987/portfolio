lender-be

A fullstack group project for DigitalCrafts, Lender-Be is a site for lenders and borrowers to connect and lend or borrow anything they might have around the house that someone would be willing to lend or need to borrow. (With thanks to Shakespeare/Polonius for the name.) We used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, Node.JS, and Express. Check out the gitHub and youTube video.
